1. What STLTECH Does (post-demerger)

After the 2025 demerger of its services arm (now STLNETWORK), Sterlite Technologies is a pure optical fiber and cables manufacturer:

  • Optical fiber (OFC) manufacturing — India's largest domestic capacity
  • Optical fiber cables for telecom, 5G, data centers
  • Network equipment and connectivity solutions

Revenue geography: India + exports (Europe, US, APAC).

2. The Core Problem — China

This is the main thesis breaker. China subsidises OFC manufacturing and has been dumping at prices Indian manufacturers cannot match. Global OFC prices fell ~50%+ from peak.

6. Relationship with STLNETWORK

Both entities came from the same parent (Sterlite Technologies pre-2025 demerger).

  • STLTECH = products (fiber manufacturing) — larger, more competitive, loss-making
  • STLNETWORK = services (telecom deployment) — smaller, debt-heavy, also loss-making
  • See STLNETWORK.md for the services entity (which is the user's actual holding)

If forced to choose today: STLNETWORK at P/B 1.05× has less downside than STLTECH at P/B 4.44×. But neither deserves fresh capital until fundamentals inflect.
