1. Business Summary

India's largest digital investment platform by active users on NSE. Offers stocks, derivatives, mutual funds, bonds, margin trading, and personal loans via a direct-to-customer model. Founded by ex-Flipkart employees. IPO in Nov 2025. The core thesis is India's massive underpenetrated financial services market being captured by a tech-first platform.

3. Why This Could Be a Multi-Bagger

  • Catalyst 1: India demat account explosion. ~15Cr demat accounts for 140Cr population (~10%). If this doubles in 5 years, Groww captures disproportionate share as largest platform.
  • Catalyst 2: Revenue diversification. Currently broking-heavy. MF distribution, lending (personal loans), and margin trading are higher-margin, recurring revenue.
  • Catalyst 3: Operating leverage. Platform costs are largely fixed. OPM went from -55% (FY22) → 62% (FY25).
  • Catalyst 4: Young user base. Average Groww user is 25-30 years old. Lifetime value is very high as AUM compounds over decades.
