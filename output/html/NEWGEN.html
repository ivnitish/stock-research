1. Business Summary

Indian software product company (not services). Builds low-code/no-code platforms for workflow automation, document management, and BPM (Business Process Management). Sells to banks, insurers, and government across 70+ countries. This is a genuine product business with recurring SaaS-like revenue — rare among Indian IT companies.

Key distinction: Newgen is a product company, not an IT body shop. Its platform competes with ServiceNow, Pegasystems, and Appian globally. Indian cost base + global product = structural margin advantage.
