Why Hold (Not Exit)

  • Only scaled US rare earth miner. Mountain Pass mine in California — only operating rare earth mine outside China at scale. No substitute exists in the US.
  • China RE export ban tailwind. China controls ~70% of global rare earth processing. Ongoing US-China trade war has put rare earths in focus. US DoD has been actively funding domestic RE supply chain — MP is the obvious beneficiary.
  • EV magnet opportunity. NdFeB magnets (permanent magnets for EV motors, wind turbines) require rare earths. MP is building a magnet manufacturing facility (Fort Worth, TX). If it secures GM/Ford/Tesla contracts, the business re-rates from mining → materials → magnets (higher margin).

Key Concerns

  • Pre-profits on the magnet business. Revenue is growing but margins are volatile with rare earth spot prices.
  • China can flood the market with RE supply to undercut US producers — price war risk.
  • Execution risk on magnet manufacturing ramp.
  • Thin float, stock is volatile.
