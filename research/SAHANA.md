# SAHANA — Sahana System Ltd

**Verdict: AVOID** | **Date: 2026-07-19** | **CMP: ~₹892 (NSE SME)** | **MCap: ~₹800 Cr**

Not a re-buy candidate at any current price. This isn't a valuation call — the accounting quality itself is the reason.

## Summary Verdict

Reported financials look world-class on the screen — sales ₹24 Cr → ₹331 Cr over three years, PAT ₹6 Cr → ₹75 Cr, ROE 31.7%, ROCE 39.8%, P/E 10-11x. Under the hood: **FCF negative both FY25 (−₹61 Cr) and FY26 (−₹6 Cr)**, OPM contracting from 40% to 30% as sales went 14x (anti-operating-leverage), promoter holding dropping 62.9% → 57.3%, business scope drifting from IT services into hardware trading + defence + agri govt tenders. The 1:5 bonus + mainboard migration approved in June 2026 read as a shareholder-friendly catalyst in a light-touch pass; on the full deep-dive they read as the classic SME pump/exit cluster.

## Kill signals hit (framework Phase 4.5.5)

1. **Structural margin compression as revenue scales** — 40% → 30% OPM over the same period sales went 14x
2. **FCF chronically below reported PAT** — receivables-financed growth
3. **Promoter dilution + bonus + rights + mainboard migration cluster** — SME pump signature
4. **Heterogeneous unrelated segments** in a 5-year-old company (IT + hardware trading + defence + agri)
5. **Prior promoter venture abandoned** without disclosure
6. **SME listing** — weaker disclosure, easier price manipulation, poor liquidity

## Business scope drift (biggest flag)

Company positions itself as "CMMI Level-5 DeepTech" IT services firm doing AI/ML, web/mobile, cloud, cybersecurity. But financials also include:
- Trading of franking machines, laptops, LED equipment
- Defence subsidiary (anti-drone, radar) — ₹24.69 Cr order from CEL
- Agri digitization — ₹70 Cr govt order

Revenue mix 65% government, 35% private. A ₹300 Cr, 5-year-old company doing five unrelated things is the SME microcap pattern, not the exception.

## What breaks the AVOID (i.e. what would we need to see to reconsider)

Two consecutive years of positive FCF matching reported PAT, promoter holding stable or rising, business segments consolidated into one focus area, mainboard listing with institutional coverage picking it up. None of these are proximate. Revisit only if all three conditions hold post-2028.
