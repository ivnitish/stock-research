## 1. Origin — Demerger from Sterlite Technologies (2025)

STL Networks was carved out of Sterlite Technologies in 2025. The demerger separated:

| Entity | Business | Listed As |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| Sterlite Technologies (STLTECH) | Optical fiber product manufacturing | STLTECH |
| STL Networks (brand: Invenia) | Telecom infrastructure services / deployment | STLNETWORK |

**Demerger ratio:** 1 STLNETWORK share for every 1 STLTECH share held.

The logic: manufacturing and services have very different economics. Separating them allows independent capital allocation and clearer valuation.

---

## 2. What STL Networks Does

A telecom infrastructure services company. Revenue comes from:
- Fiber network rollout and deployment
- System integration for telecom / data center networks
- Network operations and maintenance

**Business model:** Project-based EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction). Revenue is lumpy — recognised on project completion. Margins are thin (4-6% OPM typical for this segment).

**Customers:** Telecom operators, government infra projects (BharatNet), data center operators.
