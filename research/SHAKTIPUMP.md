## 1. Business Summary

Manufacturer of pumps, motors, and controllers. Primary catalyst: **PM-KUSUM scheme** — Government of India's ₹34,000 Cr program to solarize 35 lakh agricultural pumps. Shakti is a leading beneficiary as India's largest solar pump manufacturer. Also serves industrial, domestic, and export markets.

**The thesis:** Government scheme creates guaranteed order flow. Solar pump conversion from diesel is irreversible (energy savings are too compelling). ROCE of 55% means every rupee of government capital deployed generates exceptional returns.

## 4. Key Risk: Scheme Dependency

PM-KUSUM is 70%+ of revenue. If the scheme is delayed (budget cuts, political change), revenue can drop sharply — FY23 showed this (revenue fell from ₹1,179Cr to ₹968Cr when scheme disbursements slowed).

**Mitigation:** Industrial + export diversification growing. Government has committed to scheme through 2026.
