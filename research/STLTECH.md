## 1. What STLTECH Does (post-demerger)

After the 2025 demerger of its services arm (now STLNETWORK), Sterlite Technologies is a pure **optical fiber and cables manufacturer**:

- Optical fiber (OFC) manufacturing — India's largest domestic capacity
- Optical fiber cables for telecom, 5G, data centers
- Network equipment and connectivity solutions

**Revenue geography:** India + exports (Europe, US, APAC).

---

## 2. The Core Problem — China

This is the main thesis breaker. China subsidises OFC manufacturing and has been dumping at prices Indian manufacturers cannot match. Global OFC prices fell ~50%+ from peak.

| Metric | FY23 (peak) | FY25 (current) | Change |
|--------|------------|----------------|--------|
| Revenue | ₹6,925 Cr | ₹3,996 Cr | -42% |
| Net Profit | ₹127 Cr | -₹123 Cr | Loss |
| OPM | 12% | 10% | -2pp |
| Market share (ex-China OFC) | ~12% | ~8% | Shrinking |

Revenue has nearly halved in 2 years. This is not a cyclical dip — structural pricing pressure from Chinese overcapacity.

## 4. When Would STLTECH Become Interesting?

All three conditions must be met before considering a buy:

| Condition | Current Status | Watch For |
|-----------|---------------|-----------|
| Revenue stabilises | Declining (-10% CAGR) | Two consecutive quarters of growth |
| Returns to profit | Net loss -₹123 Cr FY25 | Full year positive PAT |
| Valuation normalises | P/B 4.44× | P/B below 1.5-2× (CMP ~₹80-100 range) |

**Key policy catalyst:** India's DGTR (Directorate General of Trade Remedies) anti-dumping duty petition on Chinese OFC imports. If granted, STLTECH re-rates sharply. This is the single most important thing to track.
