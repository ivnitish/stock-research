## Why this is a monitor position

Southwest Pinnacle Exploration is a small-cap oil & gas services company — onshore drilling, work-over rigs, mining exploration, and geo-technical services for clients including ONGC, Oil India, Vedanta-Cairn, and Coal India. The thesis hook is the domestic exploration push — DGH and MoPNG have been pressing for higher domestic crude output, the OALP rounds are bringing more acreage online, and any sustained increase in onshore drilling activity flows directly to specialist service providers. Interesting to track because it's a tiny operator in a sector where order book visibility can change quickly, and the asset base (rigs) is the binding capacity constraint — utilisation and day-rate trends are the readable signal.

## What to watch before researching properly

- Quarterly order inflow from ONGC / Oil India / private E&P operators
- Rig utilisation % and day rates — disclosed in concalls and presentations
- Working capital days and receivables aging — PSU clients can stretch payments
- Promoter holding and any pledge changes — small-cap governance basics
