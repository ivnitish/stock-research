## Bull Case

Railway theme is one of the highest-conviction structural stories in India — Vande Bharat rollout, dedicated freight corridors, urban metro capex, Bullet Train phase-1. RDSO-certified suppliers with own foundry and CNC machining are a small club. Yoshi has:
- 27 products certified, 51 in pipeline — locked-in future revenue as approvals convert
- Rail Coach Factory Kapurthala CCA approval valid till 2030
- Kanpur plant coming online for bogies + couplers (high-value, high-spec products)
- 70% capacity utilization = 30% growth headroom without new capex

If Kanpur commissions on schedule (H2 FY27) and Vande Bharat + freight capex continues, revenue could go 3x from here in 3-4 years while OPM holds at 28-30%. That's the base case for 3-4x. Add PE re-rating to 30x (industry-average) and it's 4-5x.

## Bear Case

Small-cap railway castings has seen this movie before. Titagarh Wagons and Jupiter Wagons both looked like this shape in 2022-23 — huge order books, high ROE, PE re-rating — then saw 40-60% drawdowns as order flow lumpiness became visible. Neetu Yoshi is smaller and less proven than either at their peak.

Specific bear triggers:
- Debtor days above 130 in FY27 Q1 = the receivables story becomes real
- Capacity utilization stays below 75% for two more quarters = new plant is idle
- OPM drops below 25% on unfavorable order mix
- Related-party transactions disclosed with promoter entities
- Warrants converted below FMV
- Delay in Kanpur commissioning beyond H2 FY27
