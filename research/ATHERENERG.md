## Why this is a monitor position

Ather Energy is an Indian electric two-wheeler maker (450 series scooters, Rizta family-scooter) that listed in May 2025. It's a vertically integrated EV player — own batteries, own software stack, own fast-charging network (Ather Grid) — competing primarily with Ola Electric, TVS iQube, and Bajaj Chetak in the premium electric scooter segment. Interesting enough to monitor because the EV two-wheeler category is still <10% of total two-wheeler sales in India and Ather has a defensible product-software moat among the post-IPO listed pure-plays. Equally, the business is loss-making, FAME subsidy dynamics are unstable, and competitive intensity from Bajaj/TVS could compress unit economics before profitability is reached.

## What to watch before researching properly

- Monthly registration volumes vs Ola Electric and TVS iQube — is market share stable, growing, or losing?
- Gross margin trajectory per vehicle — moving toward positive contribution margin?
- Cash burn rate vs IPO proceeds — how many quarters of runway at current burn?
- FAME-III / EV subsidy policy direction — material driver of unit economics
