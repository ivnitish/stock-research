## The PE Band Framework

The most reliable single metric for Nifty valuation. Historical average PE is **20–22x**.

| Nifty P/E | Zone | Signal | Historical examples |
|-----------|------|--------|---------------------|
| Below 16x | **Deep value** | Back up the truck | 2008 crash, Mar 2020 COVID |
| 16–20x | **Undervalued** | Invest aggressively | Bear markets, major corrections |
| 20–22x | **Fair value** | Selective — buy quality | Current zone (Apr 2026) |
| 22–25x | **Slightly expensive** | Be picky, hold cash | 2023–24 bull run |
| Above 25x | **Expensive** | Trim, don't deploy fresh | Jan 2024 Nifty peak |

**Current PE 21x = fair value.** Not a screaming buy, not a sell signal.

## The Three Metrics to Monitor

### 1. Nifty PE vs Historical Average

- **Below 18x** → Strong buy signal, deploy aggressively
- **18–20x** → Good time, buy quality
- **20–22x** → Neutral, be selective (current)
- **Above 24x** → Caution, demand higher margin of safety

### 2. Nifty from 52-Week High

The index level masks individual stock pain. Nifty at -9% from peak hides the fact that many quality mid/smallcaps are -30% to -67% from their highs.

**How to use:** If Nifty is -15%+ from peak AND PE is below 18x, that's a generational buying zone. At -9% from peak and 21x PE, the index itself is fine — individual stock selection matters more.

### 3. P/B Ratio

| P/B | Zone |
|-----|------|
| Below 2.5x | Cheap |
| 2.5–3.5x | Fair (current: 3.3x) |
| Above 4.0x | Expensive |
| Above 4.5x | Extreme — historical sell zone |

Current P/B 3.3x = slightly below midpoint of fair range.

## When Is Nifty a Screaming Buy?

All three conditions together:
1. PE below 17x AND
2. Index more than 20% off peak AND
3. News flow at maximum fear (COVID-style, systemic crisis)

The last such window was March 2020 (PE ~16x, Nifty -38% from peak). Nifty Apr 2026 at 21x / -9% from peak is **not** that window — but individual stocks often offer similar opportunity even when the index doesn't.
