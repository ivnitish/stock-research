## Phase 1 — Business Overview

### What Grab Does
Grab is Southeast Asia's leading superapp — a single platform integrating ride-hailing, food delivery, grocery, fintech (GrabFin), and enterprise services. Operates across 8 countries: Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines, Cambodia, Myanmar.

**Three Segments:**
- **Deliveries** (food, grocery, mart): 51% of FY2024 revenue ($1.10B). GMV $8.3B. Take rate ~13.3%
- **Mobility** (ride-hailing): 29% of FY2024 revenue ($0.63B). GMV $3.3B. Take rate 19.1%
- **Financial Services (GrabFin)**: 13% of FY2024 revenue ($0.28B) — fastest growing; digital banking (GXS Bank Singapore, GX Bank Malaysia), lending, insurance, payments

**Revenue Model:** Platform take rates on GMV + lending interest income + insurance premiums + enterprise B2B services

## Phase 3 — Market Opportunity

### TAM
- SEA digital economy GMV: $300B (2025) growing to $1T by 2030 (Google/Temasek/Bain e-Conomy SEA 2025)
- SEA ride-hailing: $16B (2024) growing to $28B (2030), CAGR 10%
- SEA food delivery: $20B growing to $37B, CAGR 11%
- SEA digital financial services: $39B growing to $110B, CAGR 23% — highest growth vertical

### Penetration Gap vs Developed Markets
- Digital payments penetration SEA: 52% vs 90%+ in developed markets
- 400M+ adults underbanked or unbanked across SEA — GrabFin's structural tailwind
- Online food delivery: 15-20% penetration vs 35%+ in China/Korea

### The Flywheel
Rides -> food -> payments -> lending -> insurance. Each product feeds data into the next. This is Grab's compounding engine.

- **AirAsia MOVE / Maxim**: Niche players; not at scale to threaten core markets

## Phase 5 — Reinvestment Runway

### GrabFin — The Re-rating Story
- GXS Bank (Singapore, launched 2023): $1B loan book milestone hit FY2025
- GX Bank (Malaysia, launched 2024): Fastest-growing digital bank in Malaysia
- BNPL, insurance, wealth products — early stage, high optionality
- Grab processes $22.9B GMV/year — natural funnel for financial products
- If GrabFin reaches 25% of revenue at higher margins: significant earnings upgrade catalyst

### GoTo Merger (Binary Catalyst)
- If approved: Grab gains dominant Indonesia position (270M population, SEA's largest economy)
- Indonesia was Grab's most contested market due to GoTo competition
- Combined entity would be #1 across all 8 SEA markets
- GoTo financially strained — Grab has the stronger hand in negotiations
- Status: KPPU review ongoing, decision expected H1 2026
